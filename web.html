Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on complexity. A simple business site can take 1-2 weeks; a custom platform 1-4 weeks. We’ll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Can I update the website myself after it’s built?

Yes. For WordPress sites, you get an easy editor. For custom sites, we can add a simple CMS or provide training.

Do you offer maintenance and support?

Absolutely. We offer monthly plans that include updates, backups, security, and 24/7 monitoring.

Will my website be mobile‑friendly?

Always. Every site we build is fully responsive and tested on all devices.
