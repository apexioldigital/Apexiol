Our Development Process

01

Discovery & Requirements

We interview your team, study your current workflows, and document exactly what the software must do. No assumptions, just clarity.

02

Architecture & Design

We create database schemas, API contracts, and technical specifications. You review everything before a single line of code is written.

03

Agile Development

We build in sprints. You see working software every two weeks and give feedback. Changes are welcomed, not penalised.

04

Testing & Quality Assurance

Automated unit tests, integration tests, and manual QA. We also provide a staging environment for your team to test.

05

Deployment & DevOps

We deploy to your chosen infrastructure cloud, VPS, or on‑premise. We set up CI/CD pipelines for smooth updates and rollbacks.

06

Training & Support

We train your staff, provide documentation, and offer ongoing maintenance, bug fixes, and feature enhancements.

Where Your Software Runs

Cloud AWS Azure GCP

Best for scalability and global access. You pay for what you use. Auto‑scaling, backups, and monitoring included.

Virtual Private Server VPS

Affordable and powerful. Hostinger VPS, DigitalOcean, Linode. Full root access, great for most businesses.

On‑Premise

Your own servers inside your office. Ideal for sensitive data, compliance healthcare or finance, or air‑gapped environments.

Platform as a Service PaaS

Heroku, Railway, Render. Simplest option – you push code, and the platform manages the servers automatically.

We help you choose the right infrastructure based on your budget, security needs, and expected load. We also handle the entire deployment process.

Automated Deployment Pipeline

Every code change goes through automated tests, builds, and deployment – so updates are safe and fast.

Common Questions

How long does it take to build custom web software?

It depends on complexity. A simple internal tool can take 4 to 6 weeks. A full ERP system might take 3 to 6 months. We give you a clear timeline upfront.

Can I request changes during development?

Absolutely. We use agile methodology, so you see progress every two weeks and can adjust priorities as your business evolves.

Do I need to manage servers?

No. We handle deployment, hosting setup, backups, and security. You just use the software. If you prefer on‑premise, we can set that up too.

Will the software work on mobile browsers?

Yes. All our web software is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops – no separate app needed.
